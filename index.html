Build Planner 5e



Build Planner 5e is a web application designed to help players of Dungeons And Dragons 5th Edition to plan, build, and maintain their player characters. The application allows players to plan ahead and build a solid vision of what their player character will turn out to be and doubles as a mobile character sheet that can be used while playing, circumventing the age old blunder of leaving your sheets at home.

Gnolab



Gnolab is a puzzle platformer developed for the October 2nd Ludum Dare game jam. Based on the prompt, Unstable, the game follows a Gnome in a laboratory, completing puzzles by collecting ingredients and combining them to make potions with wildly differing effects. Gnolab was finished within 48 hours and placed in the top 50% overall out of 3000 submissions. It is available to play here.
